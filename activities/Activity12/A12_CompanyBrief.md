## 3. Our Story — About Us Section

Patitas Chic was born out of a simple conviction: dogs are family, and family deserves style.

It all started in 2019 when two childhood friends from Mérida — **Valentina Cruz Montejo** and **Rodrigo Acuña Herrera** — decided to combine their passions into a business. Valentina had just returned from Mexico City after earning her fashion design degree from CENTRO (Centro de Diseño, Cine y Televisión), and Rodrigo, a practicing veterinarian with eight years of experience, was looking for a way to merge animal welfare with something more creative.

The result was Patitas Chic: a boutique that designs handcrafted dog fashion inspired by Yucatán's rich textile heritage — the embroidery of the Mayan huipil, the elegance of the guayabera, and the vivid colors of the Yucatán landscape — all made with 100% pet-safe, hypoallergenic materials.

What began as a small workshop in their families' hacienda-style home in the Centro Histórico has grown into one of Mexico's most recognized dog fashion brands, shipping to all 32 states and internationally to the United States and Canada.

**Our values:**
- 🐾 **Pet safety first** — every fabric and dye is vetted by Rodrigo and is non-toxic and hypoallergenic
- 🌿 **Sustainability** — we use certified eco-friendly fabrics including henequén blends and organic cotton
- 🤝 **Local artisans** — we work with Mayan embroidery artisans from Ticul and Maní to create our hand-embroidered pieces
- ❤️ **Shelter support** — 5% of every sale goes to Huellas de Amor, a local dog rescue shelter in Mérida

## 8. FAQ (for Contact / Footer section)

**Q: Do you ship outside Mexico?**
A: Yes! We currently ship to the United States and Canada. International orders are processed within 2 business days and typically arrive in 7–12 business days via DHL Express.

**Q: How do I find my dog's size?**
A: Each product page includes a size guide. We recommend measuring your dog's chest girth, neck girth, and back length. If between sizes, size up for comfort.

**Q: Are your materials safe for dogs?**
A: Absolutely. All fabrics, dyes, and hardware are reviewed by our in-house veterinarian, Rodrigo Acuña. We use only hypoallergenic, non-toxic materials.

**Q: Can I return or exchange an item?**
A: Yes — within 15 days of delivery for unworn items. Exchanges are free on your first request.
